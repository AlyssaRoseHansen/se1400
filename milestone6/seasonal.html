Sesonal/Holiday Adventures at Wild Oasis

Spring


Spring is a fan-favorite time to visit. Our flowers and trees are in full bloom and the birds are churpping.


Spring Garden


Our spring garden is full of bulbed flowers such as tulips, Daffodils, Anemones, Hyainths, Lilies and more. And, for classic spring, our color theme is pinks, purples, whites, and light greens. Come enjoy the smells and colors of the season.


Easter

Come join us on the week of easter. We have several different hidden easter eggs for you to find. Don't forget to ask for our scavenger sheet at the front gate. We also have the Easter bunny come visit the park from 12-2 Monday through Saturday for family photos.


Summer


Come beat the heat under the shade.


Summer Garden


Our summer garden is bright and colorful to allumiate the fun of summer. It consists of Cosmos, Dahlias, Coneflowers, Zinnias, and more. But don't worry, we have several large shade trees with benches underneath for you to escape the heat.


Fourth Of July


We transform the heart of our Gardens into a red, white and blue field. And on the 4th we have our very own Firework show.


Fall


Come enjoy the colors of fall as our gardens turn into a beautiful canopy of reds, oranges, and yellows.


Fall Garden


In the theme of fall colors, our Fall Garden is filled with reds, oranges and yellows. With Dahlias, Sunflowers, and pansys we help the seasons magic come alive.


Halloween


Come Trick-or-Treat with us! Every Friday and Saturday in the month of October from 6pm to 9pm we host a Trick-or-Treat night. With stations in each garden and around our park we have so much candy to give. Make sure you buy your tickets early before we sell out.


We also have a Halloween scavenger hunt for you and the family to enjoy. Make sure you grab our scavenger sheet at the front gate. And keep your eyes out for our witches, bats, brooms and more!


Winter


Winter can be cold, so grab a hot chocolate from our cafe and bundle up for our winter spectacular.


Winter Garden


Winter doesn't mean everything is dormant. And in our Winter Garden we prove that. With Helebores, Cyclamen, Ornamental Kales, and more we have a beautiful garden of white, purples, and blues.



Thanksgiving


We offer a Turkey Scavenger hunt! We have hidden turkeys all over our gardens. See if you can find them all. Don't forget to ask for our scavenger sheet at the front gate.


December


For the entire month of December our gardens are illuminated in lights. We offer a holiday scavanger hunt with candy canes, hidden Santa, elves, draddels and more! Pick up your scavenger sheet at the front gate.

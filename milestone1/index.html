Website Summary

What Content will the site contain?

The site will have information about our garden center. It will contain photos of the gardens, information about activities, and give information about events. It will contain a map of the properties that will show the diffent attractions. It will also have information about hosting events, how to contact, ticket purchasing, and important dates.

Where will you get all of the content for your site?

I have a lot of photos I have taken that I can use. I will also find stock photos and may create a map if needed.

Why should users care about the focus of the site?

They should care because it keeps them up to date with what is happening at the gardends. It also helps users get the most out of their experience.

Why should a site be built for this?

This sight should be built because it will help inspire people to visit the gardens, know what will be going on, and help visitors get the most out of their visit. Having a site like this will increase visitors.

What would you like users to do at your site?

I want them to explore it and see the different oportunities we offer. I want them to get excited by this and purchase tickets. Maybe even buy a tour or book our gardens for their events.

What is the goal of the site?

To inspire and excite our future guests to come to our gardens and help then want to keep coming back.
